Tobby Hagler
Demeter's not herself anymore.

Once the pride of humanity's interstellar fleet, the ESS Demeter is on a final voyage to the forests of Tau Ceti. On board, a haunted telepath, an ancient Druid, and a sentient sapling hold the key to reawakening a dying world.

When psychic interference stirs something hidden in Demeter's organic systems, an unsettling truth emerges — one that reshapes everything the crew believed about their mission, their ship, and themselves.

With time running out, they face an impossible choice: reclaim what was lost, or risk losing the galaxy's last living connection to Earth.

About the Author
Tobby Hagler is a software engineering director and speculative-fiction author exploring the intersections of technology, humanity, and the unknown. He lives with his wife and their three dogs in the Carolina Midlands, beside a quiet pond and a nearly unhaunted forest. The Grove of Tau Ceti is his first novel.
