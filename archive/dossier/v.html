Specialist · Organic-Mechanical Systems Integration · ESS Demeter
Specialist Violet Sandoval enlisted after leaving Rimeholt and was assigned to ESS Demeter for organic-mechanical systems integration. Born in Galway and trained around Druidic rootwork, she can feel the vessel’s aches through loquentes systems with a precision most technicians mistake for intuition. She rejects the title Aine Sandoval and prefers V, a name with private family weight. Druidic affiliation is disclosed and legal; willingness to use chaint remains conditional, volatile, and mission-critical.
Field Annotations

Sandoval has not chainted voluntarily in years and identifies the ability as a curse. Her stated fear centers on Ris Sandoval, whose final moments remain an open wound in both family and Druidic records. Counseling Flag · Druidic Practice Review
