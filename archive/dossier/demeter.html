ESS Demeter
FF-513
Fleet Frigate · Tau Ceti Transit · Diplomatic Mission
ESS Demeter FF-513 is a pre-Alliance Fleet frigate whose organic conscience and mechanical AI were grown into the ship long before newer command doctrine made that arrangement unfashionable. Her rootwork assists navigation, crew assignment, environmental control, and the thousand quiet accommodations that turn an old hull into a home. The Tau Ceti transit is her final diplomatic mission before scheduled decommissioning: deliver humanity’s portion of the gravitational wave array, keep a mixed crew breathing the same compromised air, and prove the old vessel still has one more crossing in her.
