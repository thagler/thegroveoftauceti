BEKHTI, LILIAN
Commander · Executive Officer · ESS Demeter
Commander Lilian Bekhti holds the post of Executive Officer aboard ESS Demeter, serving as second in command with primary authority over crew operations and internal discipline. She was appointed to the position by Captain Hargreaves at the outset of the Tau Ceti mission. Her administrative record reflects consistent commendation for operational coordination and command advisement.
