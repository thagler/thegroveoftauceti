Bekhti, Lillian
Commander · Executive Officer / Chief Navigator / Boatswain · ESS Demeter
Commander Lillian Bekhti holds the post of Executive Officer aboard ESS Demeter, serving as second in command with operational authority over crew coordination, navigation, and shipboard discipline. Her current posting combines boatswain, executive officer, and chief navigator responsibilities, an overload that would be irregular on a newer ship and apparently routine on this one. Bekhti’s command style is clipped, exacting, and practical; she is often the person translating Captain Hargreaves’s instincts into trajectories, rosters, and executable orders.
