Hargreaves, John
Captain · Commanding Officer · ESS Demeter
Captain of Record, ESS Demeter FF-513. Hargreaves has served aboard or alongside Demeter across multiple career stages, from hopeful junior officer to the command chair now responsible for the Tau Ceti diplomatic transit. He is an old-school captain in a ship that has long since learned to make decisions without him, and the tension appears to keep both parties honest. Registry notes repeatedly identify him as protective of the entire mixed crew, including personnel whose cultures, physiologies, and gods make his coffee worse.
