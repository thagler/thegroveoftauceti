Section Chief · Engineering / Signal Systems · ESS Demeter
Section Chief Eugene Parker came to Demeter after years of short-haul inter-Solar Fleet work and at least one failed attempt to get into the small-ship pilot pipeline. He knows the old vessel’s neglected systems with alarming intimacy, has pursued nearly every training certification in her library, and has been restoring the obsolete T-12 dropship Arion with Hector during off-hours. Command notes describe him as useful, funny, evasive, and not always clear on the boundary between informal maintenance and unauthorized infrastructure.
Field Annotations
