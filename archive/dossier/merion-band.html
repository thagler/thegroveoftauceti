About
Merion was a volatile Earth band whose catalog survived Fleet jumps, Druidic arboretums, teenage bedrooms, and the ESS Demeter’s worst coffee. Public records and crew recollections describe a meteoric rise, a drug-fueled collapse, and comeback tours that made the old songs feel less like nostalgia than a dare. Specialist Sandoval and Doctor Hawthorne independently identify as committed fans; this is the only known subject on which their initial rapport required no translation.
