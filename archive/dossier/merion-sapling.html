Recovered from Brigid during the Tau Ceti f survey after an entity identified by Specialist Sandoval as the Old Man of the Forest directed her attention to a struggling sapling. Sandoval named the specimen Merion under Druidic naming logic: names create responsibility, and responsibility does not appear to expire. Merion communicates through imagery, simple words, taps against its sample container, and behavioral responses inconsistent with non-sentient flora. Classification as sentient entity is recommended by Specialist Sandoval and Doctor Hawthorne; Fleet administrative registry continues to object to the paperwork.
Field Annotations
