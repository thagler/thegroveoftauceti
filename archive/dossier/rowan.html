Forsythe, Rowan

Lieutenant · Acting Section Chief, Engineering · ESS Demeter
Lieutenant Rowan Forsythe reenlisted for the Tau Ceti transit after prior service at Jupiter Station and subsequent long-haul engineering work. Following a temporary Engineering command vacancy, Captain Hargreaves appointed Forsythe acting section chief, citing his ability to read impossible telemetry while also noticing every loose ballast and broken chair. He is a reluctant leader, a precise systems engineer, and one of the few non-Druids trusted by Specialist Sandoval when Demeter’s organic systems behave like something more than machinery.
