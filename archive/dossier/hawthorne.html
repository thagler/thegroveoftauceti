Hawthorne
Known as “Thorney”
Doctor · Loquentes Systems · ESS Demeter
Assigned to ESS Demeter under Fleet Special Dispensation Order 19-H, which authorizes loquentes practitioners of demonstrated renown to serve outside standard rank hierarchy. Hawthorne is a Galway-born Druid, a public figure in telepathic science, and the designer of a loquentes interface she insists is not a tiara. Her work synchronizes neural signals with rootwork systems so organic consciousness and mechanical AI can be studied together. Informal accounts describe her as half lab coat, half contraband coffee, and entirely unwilling to let a silent ship stay silent.
