Hector
Known as “Hex”
Lieutenant · Persephone Skipper · ESS Demeter
Lieutenant Hector serves as skipper of the dropship Persephone and a field authority on launch procedures, old radios, and spacer traditions that nobody else is allowed to ignore. He and Parker maintain obsolete antennae, analog comms gear, and the half-revived Arion project with a devotion that looks suspiciously like courtship when translated through matelotage records. Hector’s personnel file is operationally strong and socially complicated: decisive under pressure, loyal to his mates, and occasionally in need of direct correction regarding Druids and allied species.
