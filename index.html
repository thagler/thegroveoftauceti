The Grove of Tau Ceti

A debut novel by Tobby Hagler

Once the pride of humanity’s interstellar fleet, the ESS Demeter nears the end of an eight-month voyage to Tau Ceti, where the promise of a new interstellar alliance awaits

Demeter's not herself anymore.

Something stirs in the shadows of her circuits and rootwork, ancient and hidden, with designs far beyond the ship’s final mission. When psychic interference from a nearby alien moon plunges Demeter into strange silence, an unsettling truth emerges: the ship’s organic systems may no longer be under their control.

A mysterious forest teeming with life—or something like it—offers both hope and dread, blurring the line between the living and the mechanical. In its shadow, a telepathic technician must confront her latent abilities and a painful past. With the help of a fellow Druid, a disgraced communications engineer, and a sapling named Merion, they fight to reclaim control of their ship. Together, the remaining crew must face an impossible choice: defend their home or risk losing the galaxy.
