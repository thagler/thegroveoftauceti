About the Book

The ESS Demeter, once the pride of humanity’s interstellar fleet, is on a final voyage to Tau Ceti and the promise of a new alliance. But when the ship’s organic systems begin to behave unpredictably—a reaction to psychic interference from a mysterious alien moon—the crew realizes something ancient and alive has taken root aboard.

Their only hope lies with a telepathic technician haunted by her past, who must reawaken her suppressed abilities. In alliance with a fellow Druid, a disgraced engineer, and a sentient sapling, she faces an impossible choice: save Demeter and all aboard—or risk unleashing a force that could reshape the galaxy.
