Demeter's not herself anymore.

Once the pride of humanity’s interstellar fleet, the ESS Demeter is on a final voyage to Tau Ceti, where the promise of a new alliance awaits. But something stirs deep within the ship’s rootwork, hidden and ancient, unfolding.

When psychic interference from a nearby alien moon plunges Demeter into strange silence, an unsettling truth emerges: the ship’s organic systems may no longer be under their control.

A telepathic technician haunted by her past must reawaken her abilities to help save Demeter. With the aid of a fellow Druid, a disgraced engineer, and a sentient sapling, the crew fights for survival against an unknown intelligence bent on reshaping them.

With time running out, the remaining crew face an impossible choice: reclaim their home or risk losing the galaxy.

About the Author

Tobby Hagler is a Software Engineering Director with extensive experience in cutting-edge web content management system development and software development training. He has presented at local and international conferences, using his ability to make complex technical concepts accessible to diverse audiences. He lives with his wife and their three dogs in the Carolina midlands, where he enjoys the quiet inspiration of his pondside home nestled in a nearly unhaunted forest.
